Contact

WDW Wayfinder is built on a living dataset of transit nodes and edges. Because Disney's transportation network — from boat routes to walking paths — is constantly evolving, your real-world observations are our most valuable resource.

If you encounter a missing route, a closed walkway, or an inaccurate timing, please let us know through our GitHub channels. Whether you're opening a formal issue or proposing a direct fix to the dataset, your contributions help keep the 'magic' optimized for the entire community.
