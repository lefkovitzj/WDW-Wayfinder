The Underlying Graph

Unlike static datasets, our network graph is programmatically generated. We use a Python-based build engine that transforms high-level transit rules (such as automated bus frequency logic) wherever possible and relies on manual override tuples to generate the rest of the transit connections that cannot be simulated with programmatic rules. The combination of the programmatic and manual approaches results in the final JSON transit map. If you're looking to contribute, you can submit PRs to our data_converter.py to refine how routes are calculated or data/raw_data.py to add new connection tuples to our source arrays. This ensures our data remains dry, scalable, and easy to validate.
